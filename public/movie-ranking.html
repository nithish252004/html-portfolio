The Best Movies According to Me

My Top 3 Moives of All Time

Jersey

Arjun, a talented but failed cricketer, decides to return to cricket in his late thirties, driven by the desire to represent the Indian cricket team and fulfil his son's wish for a jersey as a gift.

Thozha

Impressed by his carefree attitude, a quadriplegic multi-billionaire, hires a convict out on parole as his caretaker. What follows is a string of life lessons they learn from each other.

MS Dhoni

M S Dhoni, a boy from Ranchi, aspires to play cricket for India. Though he initially tries to please his father by working for the Indian Railways, he ultimately decides to chase his dreams.
